ABOUT AGGS BADMINTON

The AGGS Badminton Club brings together students with a passion for badminton, from beginners to advanced players. Our club provides a space to build skills, compete in interschool tournaments, and have fun while staying active. We value teamwork, sportsmanship, and the spirit of competition, while representing Auckland Girls' Grammar with pride.
